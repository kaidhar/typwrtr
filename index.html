Per-app tuning

App profiles

typwrtr lists every app you've dictated into. Give each one a vocabulary prompt to bias whisper toward your jargon, pick a postprocess mode for cleanup, optionally pin a different model, or disable learning for sensitive apps.

Dictate into a few apps first — they'll show up here. The foreground app you were focused on at paste time is the one we record.
